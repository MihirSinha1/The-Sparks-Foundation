ABOUT US

The Sparks Foundation® of is a non-profit organization that has fulfilled many wishes in the past years. The Foundation is dedicated to granting the most cherished wish of children between ages 3 and 18, diagnosed with a life-threatening medical condition, mostly cancer. Our mission is to help raise vital funds for cancer research .Help us raise vital funds. Join the fight. Our vision is to Stop Cancer Before It Starts!Help Us to Bring Smile on Thousands of Faces.
